GateFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

GateFit is operated by TrevBeats Multimedia. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Step count and walk data — collected via Apple HealthKit to track your airport walks, steps, distance, and calories burned.
  • Walk session data — gates walked between, route type, duration, and airport — stored securely to power your walk history and profile stats.
  • Flight information — entered manually by you to plan your workout. Flight lookups use the OpenSky Network public API and are not stored.

Health Data

GateFit requests access to Apple HealthKit to read your step count during walks. We do not sell, share, or use your health data for advertising. Health data is used solely to display your step count and calculate calories burned. We do not store raw HealthKit data on our servers.

Data Storage

Walk session summaries are stored securely using Supabase and associated with a device-level identifier, not your name or email.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and backend. Privacy Policy
  • OpenSky Network — public flight data API. No personal data is sent. Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS — app distribution. Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your walk history is retained until you delete the app or request deletion. To request data deletion contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

GateFit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will notify you by updating the date at the top of this page.
